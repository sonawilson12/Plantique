About Us

At Plantique, we believe that indoor plants are more than just decor—they’re a way to bring life, tranquility, and health into your space. Founded in 2020, our mission has always been to reconnect people with nature, one plant at a time.

We offer a carefully curated selection of indoor plants, perfect for homes, offices, and commercial spaces. Whether you're a seasoned plant parent or just starting out, our products are hand-picked to thrive in indoor environments and come with guidance to help them flourish.

What sets us apart? Quality plants, eco-conscious practices, and a commitment to customer satisfaction. Our team is passionate about plants, sustainability, and creating green spaces that elevate everyday life.
